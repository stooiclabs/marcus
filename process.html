MAINTENANCE

Conception

Every project starts with an in-depth brand discovery session and help to get a deep understanding of your business and goals. We will carry out market research, competitor analysis and making sure that we will build that product that you need.

We'll help you making roadmap, product specification and create wire frames for your product. You will fully understand the journey that your user will experience with your website or mobile application.
This is an important step for planning content and how the final result will resonate with your audience.

Planning

Design and Creative

We will create a pixel perfect version of your web site and a clickable prototype that can give you an better understanding about bot the final look and feel and your target audience to drive traffic and further establish your brand. 

We will take care about everything during development: from front-end to back end development to create your perfect mobile application. Our websites are build and fully optimized for multiple devices and screen sizes. We will make the final testings for you and go live.

Development

 Maintenance

Everything will be documented in the best way, so that you and your team will be able to take over without any effort.

In case you do not have a team or you would like to avoid to maintain the product, we can offer a maintenance mode where we will take care of everything from hosting to bug fixing.
